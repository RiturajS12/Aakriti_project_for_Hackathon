Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why Choose Aakriti as an Employer?

Aakriti offers a diverse pool of local skills, eliminating intermediaries and ensuring proximity to the products or services you need.

2. What is Aakriti's Mission?

Aakriti strives to provide a platform where all fundamental skills are valued, empowering individuals and contributing to our country's GDP.

3. Which Skills Can I Register on Aakriti?

Aakriti embraces all skills, from basic services like mehndi to advanced graphic design.

4. How Does Aakriti Differ from Other Platforms?

Unlike existing platforms with limited skill sets, Aakriti focuses on building social interactions within your local community, ensuring secure communication through live chat.

5. Is My Personal Information Secure?

Aakriti prioritizes your data security and ensures that your details are not misused or leaked.

6. What Should I Do if My Aakriti Account is Blocked?

Account blocking indicates unethical activity. To regain access, you can either re-register using a different email or request access via email to Aakriti.
